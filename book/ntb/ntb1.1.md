The integers $$ \mathbb{Z}=\{...,-2,-1,0,1,2...\} $$.

$$a,b,z \in \mathbb{Z}$$ \\
$$az=b $$ \\
We say that: \\
$$a$$ **devides** $$b$$. Write $$a \mid b $$.\\
$$a$$ is a  **divisor** of $$b$$, or $$b$$ is a **multiple** $$a$$, or $$b$$ is  **divisible** by $$a$$. \\
If $$a$$ does not devide $$b$$, then we write $$a \nmid b$$.
